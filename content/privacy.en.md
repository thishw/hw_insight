HW Insight (insight.thishw.com, "this site") respects your privacy. This policy applies to this site and the thishw.com domain family.

## 1. Information We Collect

This site does **not** operate sign-ups, comments, or contact forms that collect personally identifiable information. The following may be collected automatically for service improvement:

- **Usage data**: pages visited, time on page, clicks, and similar behavioral signals
- **Device data**: browser type, operating system, screen size, and approximate region (IP-based — full IP addresses are not stored)

## 2. Analytics (Cookies)

This site uses **Google Analytics 4** (via Google Tag Manager) for traffic analysis. It collects anonymized visit statistics through cookies, used solely to improve content.

To opt out:

- Block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
- Install the [Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## 3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site may serve ads through Google AdSense. When ads are served:

- Google and third-party ad vendors may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your visit history.
- You can opt out of personalized ads at [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com).
- See [Google's advertising policies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads) for details on third-party cookie use.

## 4. Data Retention and Sharing

- Collected statistics are retained per Google's data retention policies.
- Data collected via analytics and advertising tools may be processed on Google LLC servers in the United States.
- This site does not sell or share collected data with third parties except as required by law.

## 5. Contact

For privacy inquiries, reach out via the [Telegram channel](https://t.me/hw_insight).

## 6. Changes

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated date.
